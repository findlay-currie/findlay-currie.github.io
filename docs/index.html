01 // INTRODUCTION

About Me

I'm a Rotational Graduate Engineer at Veolia Water, based at the Seafield wastewater treatment works in Edinburgh. With a BEng (Hons) in Mechanical Engineering from Heriot-Watt University, I bring an engineering mindset to every problem I tackle.

What sets me apart is my passion for process digitisation. During my graduate rotation, I identified dozens of paper-based, manual workflows and built 16+ bespoke web applications using Google Apps Script — spanning safety compliance, procurement, operations monitoring, and workflow management.

These aren't throwaway prototypes. They're production tools used daily by site teams, with features like role-based access control, automated email workflows, real-time dashboards, and full audit trails. One system alone — my Permit to Work app — benchmarks at £150k–£300k+ against comparable commercial builds.
